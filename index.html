Who and What is Wind Blow ?

Wind Blow is the pseudo that I picked a year ago, in January 2018, when I started producing Music. I remember that the moment I downloaded FL Studio, the producing software, I had already picked it.

It just sounded good to my ear and that was enough.

Beat making is what brought me into it, I was dreaming of composing the next banger for the biggest French rappers. I quickly realized it was going to be harder than I thought.

But Then I Found My Way

I discovered Psytrance in January 2018 as well, but it's only in May that I started producing it. I was having way more fun, and I could spend days and nights on my computer trying to figure out how to make it sound good. I have no pretensions about my music, as long as it satisfies me.

Where am I today ?

Here we are, January 2019. After a Year producing on Fl Studio, I decided to switch on to Ableton Live, to actually start playing Live. My Soundcloud account doesn't look good but i'm only one year into producing, I still want to improve before actually putting anything serious online

Get That Creativity

I went out a lot to techno, psytrance, hardcore and any type of electronic events during the past year, and this world is amazing. The more I discover, the more I create.
